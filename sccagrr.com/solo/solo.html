    Starting times for all Solo events are as follows:
    • 7:00a-9:00a | Course setup - Volunteers welcome and needed
    • 8:00a-9:00a | Registration
    • 9:00a-9:20a | Rookie Walk
    • 8:15a-9:15a | Vehicle Tech Inspection
    • 9:30a | Drivers Meeting
    • 10:00a | First Car on the Course
    Entry Fees for 2025:
    • $35 for SCCA members and $50 for non-members
    • A $5 late fee will be imposed after 9:00a if registration is allowed
    • There is no charge for non-drivers (spectators or crew)
    • Pre-registration is available and is highly recommended

    SCCA Rule Books:
    • There is one copy of the SCCA Solo Rule Book at GRR Solo Events and it is used by the event staff. You may purchase your own SCCA Solo Rule book from the SCCA by calling 1-800-770-2055 or by visiting the SCCA website. We recommend you buy a rule book or download the latest copy from here. Referencing a rule book now will prevent wasted modifications and confusion down the road. Rule books provide all the information on car classing, modifications, specifications, etc.
