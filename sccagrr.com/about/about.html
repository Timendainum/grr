    • The Great River Region Sports Car Club of America (SCCA) is one of 116 regions that make up the nation’s largest motorsport membership organization. Chartered in 1966, Great River Region has promoted a variety of motorsport activities and social events.

    Members organize, conduct, promote, and participate in a variety of motorsport activities, including races, Autocross events, road rallies, and social functions.

    The Great River Region, Region 99, encompasses 13 counties in Southeastern Iowa and 6 counties in Northwestern Illinois. Major Iowa Cities included in the region are Bettendorf, Burlington, Clinton, Davenport, Fairfield, Ft. Madison, Keokuk, Mt. Pleasant, Muscatine, Ottumwa and Washington. In Illinois, cities include: Galesburg, Geneseo, Kewanee, Moline, Monmouth and Rock Island.
