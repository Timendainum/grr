    • Ready to join?

    Great! You have a few options here.
    • You can join over the phone with a credit card by calling 1-800-770-2055
    • You can join online through the national SCCA website by clicking here.
    • You can join when you attend an event! We have membership applications available at every event and we'll even take care of mailing them. Best part is, you can participate that same day!
    • You can use the 2025 Membership Application and send it to the address on the form!
    If applying online or over the phone, you'll have to know that the Great River Region number is 99.
    All entrants, workers, and guests must sign a waiver and display the wrist band provided. Anyone refusing to do so will be asked to leave.
    Dues for the GRR, in addition to the national dues, are $10 for a single, $2.50 for a spouse and $20 for a family.

    We look forward to seeing you on the course!
